Frequency Asked Questions


Q: Where do you get the data?
A: Universities, governments, and some other web sites.

Q: What is the frame rate?
A: The frame rates, as well as the resolutions, vary based on the cameras.

Q: I have cameras. Can I add my cameras to your site?
A: Not yet. We will add that feature soon.

Q: What do you do with the data? Are you tracking where I go?
A: We do not intend to indentify any individual. Thus, we do not know where you are.

Q: If I register, what do you do my data (such as my email address)?
A: Please read the privacy policy.

Q: Will you send me any email?
A: We intend to keep the email traffic low.

Q: What do the cameras see?
A: Most cameras observe traffic or outdoor scenes.

Q: Are you recording all data continuously?
A: No.

Q: Do you retrieve data from password-protected cameras?
A: Only if the camera owner gives us the password. We will not guess the password of any camera.
