Welcome to CAM²

the Continuous Analysis of Many CAMeras

CAM² is a system for analyzing streaming data built by a team of Purdue University researchers.

The new CAM² website is currently under construction. For full functionality, please visit cam2.ecn.purdue.edu.

CAM² provides access to tens of thousands of public camera streams from around the globe.

Use our image processing filters or create your own to analyze your camera footage.
